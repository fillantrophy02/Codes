This is a short story about a dragon.

Once, there lived a dragon as tiny as an ant.

One day, the dragon discovered a treasure trove in a random cave. "Gold!" it exclaimed as it rubbed the finest crowns and jewels. Unfortunately, the little dragon was too small to carry these treasures out of the trove.

The moral of the story is to do everything in moderation.
